What We Do

We here at Oundle School Tech Support aim to help the elderly population of Oundle with all their tech needs!

Our services include, but are not limited to:
  • Helping you set up Whatsapp for easier communications with your loved ones.
  • Helping you set up/ troubleshoot your e-mail.
  • Teaching you how to use the Video calling app of your choice.
  • General handheld device troubleshooting

Where To Find Us

We can be found in the Needham Building Wednesday afternoons during term-time. from 2:30-4pm
